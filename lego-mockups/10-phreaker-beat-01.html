PHREAKER BEAT 01
Canonical 8-bar drum pattern for every Acid Frog. Structure is identical for all 10K frogs — only the SAMPLES vary based on seed. This is the rhythmic backbone the bassline rides on.

Locked structure:
· Bars 1–4: 4-on-the-floor kick · clap on 2 & 4 · off-beat hats
· Bar 5 step 0: single kick (the drop) · then no kick
· Bars 5–7: bass + hats + claps (no kick — underground tension)
· Bar 8 step 9+: bass stops · 3 claps + ACID hit at step 12

Per-frog sample variations (seed-driven):
  • Kick variant: standard or breakbeat-flavored (extra ghost hits)
  • Hat variant: standard or 'dif hat'
  • Optional snare layer (~30% of frogs)
  • Optional shaker layer (~30%, picks 1 or 2)
  • Optional tambourine layer (~15%)
  • Optional wood-hit layer (~15%)
