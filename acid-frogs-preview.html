HOW IT WORKS

THE FULL DMT MECHANICS
1 · THE ELEMENT — SUPPLY FILTER
1ee7.1337.12.element · inscribed 22 Apr 2026 · id 3b6646a2…e80d7e0i0

Claims the pattern "1337" in field 12 (difficulty). A block qualifies as an Acid Frog only if its difficulty header field contains the substring 1337. Because the difficulty value is locked for all 2,016 blocks of an epoch, one qualifying difficulty = exactly 2,016 qualifying frogs.

In Bitcoin's 15-year history that's happened exactly once: epoch 941472–943487, 20 March–3 April 2026, difficulty 133793147307542.
2 · WHY FIELD 12
Field 12 (difficulty) has the same 2,016-block clustering mechanic as field 11 (bits) — difficulty is recalculated only at retarget, so the value is locked for an entire epoch. That's what gives us the "generation" structure: one qualifying difficulty = 2,016 qualifying blocks.

The difficulty number is a headline Bitcoiners actually track. When it contains 1337, it's immediately recognisable as the "leet" epoch — cultural alignment with the cypherpunk origin of Bitcoin.

4 · 8-BAR LOOP STRUCTURE (inherited from the baby-303 inscription)
· Bars 1–4: four-on-the-floor kick + bassline + off-beat hats + claps on 2 & 4
· Bar 5 step 0: single kick — the drop
· Bars 6–7: bass, hats, claps only (no kick — underground tension)
· Bar 8 step 9+: bass stops
· Bar 8 steps 12–15: three claps + the ACID final hit

Filter sweep: cutoff & resonance ramp upward across the 8 bars — classic acid build.

Audio stack uses existing on-chain inscriptions: Tone.js · kick · clap · hat · final "acid" hit sample — nothing new to inscribe for audio.
5 · ANIMATION — THE FROG DANCES ON THE HI-HAT
Sprites are 1500×500, 3 frames laid horizontally. On every hi-hat hit (4 per bar), the frog pops from rest (frame 0) → up (frame 1) → down (frame 2) → rest, giving one clean head-nod per hat. Between nods the frog sits still. The six layers — background + body (colour-shifted) + mouth + eyes (colour-shifted) + clothing + head + smoke — composite into one 500×500 canvas each frame. Animation only runs while audio plays; frog freezes in rest pose when silent.
6 · UNRELEASED TRAITS — LIVES FOR DECADES
Bitcoin's difficulty only grows. Every few years a future difficulty number will happen to contain 1337 again, adding 2,016 more pieces and unlocking traits that were coded into the renderer but never triggered by the April 2026 epoch. Examples:

· "Millennium" trait fires only if difficulty > 1,000T (unlocks circa 2035)
· "Halving Echo" fires only on blocks within 24h of a halving
· "Cosmic" fires only when block mined at exactly 13:37 UTC

This is the same mechanic Natcats advertises as "autonomous evolution." Holders get speculative upside that the collection is alive long after mint.
